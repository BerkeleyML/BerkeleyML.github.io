Summer 2018 CS189 at UC Berkeley

Midterm

Thursday, July 19, 2:40 - 4 p.m.

Logistics

  • Date and Time: Thursday, July 19, 2:40 - 4 p.m. Please arrive at 2:30 to get situated as the midterm will start right at 2:40.
  • Location: Lewis Hall, Room 100
  • 1 sheet (front and back) of hand written notes is allowed.
  • Bring your Cal ID.

Topics

All lecture materials until Tuesday's lecture (7/17) and the corresponding homeworks, discussions, and lecture notes are fair game

  • 0: Linear Regression, Features, Hyperparameters and Cross-Validation
  • 1: MLE, MAP, Bias-Variance, Gaussians
  • 2: Kernels, PCA, Optimization
  • 3: Neural Networks
